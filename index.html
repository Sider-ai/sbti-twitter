FAQ

Everything you need to know

SBTI is a humorous personality framework with 15 dimensions across 5 models (Self, Emotion, Attitude, Action, Social), producing 27 unique personality types.
AI reads your recent 20 tweets and maps them to 15 personality dimensions, then matches you to one of 27 types.
This is for entertainment. While AI analysis can capture real behavioral patterns from your tweets, it should not be taken as a scientific assessment.
Yes, the Sider browser extension is needed to safely read your public tweets for analysis.
We don't store your tweets. The extension reads them temporarily for analysis and nothing is saved.
SBTI uses 15 dimensions vs 4, analyzes actual behavior vs self-report questionnaires, and has a humorous rather than clinical tone.
